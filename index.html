MTD SafeBrowsing Tester

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

MTD SafeBrowsing Tester ("the App") is designed to help test and demonstrate mobile threat detection capabilities against phishing and malicious URLs. Protecting user privacy is fundamental to the design of the application.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.

All link analysis is performed locally on the device. URLs entered into the application are processed only for testing purposes and are not transmitted to external servers.

On-Device Processing

Security analysis performed by the App occurs entirely on-device. No browsing data, device identifiers, or personal information is shared with the developer or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with analytics providers, advertising networks, tracking technologies, or external data collection services.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised update date.
